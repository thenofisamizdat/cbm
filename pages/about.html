Our Story

From Four Artics to Industry Leader

CBM Transport is a family-run logistics firm owned and managed by Christy Bates, who brings over two decades of industry experience to the business. What began as a modest operation with just four articulated vehicles has grown into one of Ireland's most trusted transport companies.

Today, CBM operates a modern fleet of thirty artics, serving as a trusted service partner to several of Ireland's leading logistics providers. Our growth reflects our unwavering commitment to reliability, safety and customer satisfaction.

Based at Old Airport Road, Dublin, we provide comprehensive coverage throughout Ireland, the United Kingdom and mainland Europe, handling everything from short-haul distribution to longer cross-border freight operations.

Our Journey

Growing With Purpose

Early 2000s

Company Founded

Christy Bates establishes CBM Transport with a small fleet of four articulated vehicles, bringing years of industry experience to the new venture.

Mid 2000s

ADR Certification

CBM achieves full ADR certification, expanding capabilities to include the transport of hazardous materials including oxidising, toxic and flammable goods.

2010s

Major Grocery Partnerships

CBM becomes one of the leading subcontractors to multiple large grocery chains in Ireland, establishing a strong reputation in chilled transport.

2020s

Fleet Expansion

Growing to a fleet of 30 modern artics with comprehensive coverage across Ireland, the UK and Europe. Continuing to set the standard in reliable transport.

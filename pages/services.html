Hazardous Materials

ADR Transport

CBM Transport is a specialist in ADR (European Agreement concerning the International Carriage of Dangerous Goods by Road) transport services. Our certified drivers and purpose-equipped fleet ensure the safe, compliant transport of hazardous materials across all jurisdictions.

  • Oxidising substances
  • Toxic materials
  • Corrosive substances, acids & alkalis
  • Flammable goods
  • Full regulatory compliance across Ireland, UK & EU

About CBM

Family-Run. Industry-Leading.

Owned and managed by Christy Bates, CBM Transport has grown from a small fleet of four artics to an impressive, well-maintained modern fleet of thirty. With over two decades of industry experience, we've become a trusted service partner to several of Ireland's leading logistics providers.

We are one of the leading subcontractors to multiple large grocery chains in Ireland, and our specialist ADR and chilled transport capabilities set us apart in the industry.
